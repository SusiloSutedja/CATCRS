About Catcrs

Platform Built on Principles

Catcrs was initiated in October 2020 with a single commitment: to build a crypto asset exchange where security and transparency are not features — they are the foundation.

"Culture as System, Trust as Productivity"

Our long-term pledge is "security first, compliance as the foundation, transparency and verifiability, and practical usability." We apply engineering and governance to transform this philosophy into capabilities that users and institutions can directly perceive — not just promises that appear in marketing materials.

Core Values

What We Stand For

Four principles guide every decision we make — in engineering, compliance, product design, and communications.

🔐

Security First

Security is never traded off for speed or convenience. Default-secure configurations, hardware key management, and mandatory multi-factor authentication apply to all accounts without exception.

📋

Compliance as Foundation

Compliance is a prerequisite for operation, not a constraint we work around. We complete regulatory requirements before entering new markets — not after.

🔭

Transparent & Verifiable

We make our operations visible. Status pages, proof-of-reserves methodology, incident timelines, and root cause summaries give external parties the ability to evaluate us — not just trust us.

⚙️

Practically Usable

Our commitments are meaningless if users cannot access them. We build interfaces that are explainable, predictable, and designed to reduce uncertainty — not amplify it.

History

Key Milestones

A consistent execution record since founding — built on the principle of "foundation first, expansion second."

October 2020
Project initiated. Technical and operational roadmap established with security and transparency as first principles.
March 2021
Alpha matching engine and strategic risk control prototype completed. Closed testing commenced.
October 21, 2021
U.S. MSB (FinCEN) registration completed. KYC/AML and sanctions screening processes implemented and operational.
February 2022
Spot trading Beta launched. Cold/hot wallet v1 deployed with address reputation scoring and rules engine on deposit and withdrawal channels.
May 2022
Status page trial launched. External event timeline template established for standardized incident communication.
August 2022
First institutional market makers onboarded. API v1 released. Integrated matching and risk control mechanisms fully formed.
March 2023
Market integrity rulebook expanded with manipulation detection and abnormal volatility circuit breakers. Delisting and disclosure templates improved.
July 2023
Educational and anti-scam content library launched with multilingual coverage across all key languages.
October 2023
Matching engine v2 and API v3 (including FIX protocol) launched. Significant improvements in latency and order sequence consistency metrics.
May 2024
Multi-region disaster recovery drills normalized and standardized. Rollback procedures fully rehearsed and documented.
October 2024
HSM and multi-signature strategies upgraded. Abnormal withdrawal delays and secondary confirmations deployed for major assets. Institutional settlement tools upgraded.
May 2025
Status page transparency upgraded. Standardized display of major event timelines and root cause summaries. Compliance partner network expanded.
October 2025
Internal Proof-of-Reserves (PoR) trial completed with method review. Disclosure baseline and improvement list established.
