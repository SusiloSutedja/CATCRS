04 — Fees & Market Integrity

Transparent, Tiered Fee Structure

Our fee schedule is published on the official exchange and explained with concrete examples — designed to be short, comparable, and free of hidden terms.

Our Fee Philosophy

We implement tiered maker/taker fee rates that decrease with higher trading volume. For market makers and institutional clients, personalized arrangements are available — executed on the basis of compliance and fairness, with principles and boundaries clearly communicated. Any customized fee arrangement is documented and auditable. We do not obscure fee structures or apply undisclosed charges.
