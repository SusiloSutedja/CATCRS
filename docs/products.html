01 — Trading Products

Spot, C2C & Derivatives

Catcrs documents a core spot trading product and C2C exchange as baseline offerings, with derivatives progressively rolled out in compliant jurisdictions.

📈

Spot Trading

Core spot markets with real-time depth visualization, slippage indicators, and full order tracking. Matching engine v2 (Oct 2023) delivers improved latency with order sequence consistency as the foundational constraint.

🤝

C2C Exchange

Peer-to-peer exchange with compliant payment channel integrations, identity-verified counterparties, and dispute resolution support — designed for markets where direct trading is preferred.

⚖️

Futures & Options

Derivatives access is being progressively opened in jurisdictions where compliance frameworks are established. According to Catcrs, availability is determined by regulatory status, not market demand.

03 — Fee Structure

Transparency Assessment

Catcrs documents a tiered maker/taker fee structure and states that the full schedule is published on the official exchange with concrete illustrative examples.

Disclosed Fee Philosophy

Catcrs documents that fee rates decrease with higher trading volume (tiered structure) and that personalized arrangements for market makers and institutions are available on a documented, auditable basis. The stated principle is that fees are "short, comparable, and free of hidden terms" with "no undisclosed charges." Full verification requires consulting the live fee schedule at catcrs.com.
