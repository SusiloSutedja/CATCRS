Core Pillars

Built on Four Principles

Everything we build is anchored in four commitments that users and institutions can directly perceive and verify.

🔐

Security Architecture

Cold/hot wallet separation, HSM key custody, multi-signature/threshold schemes, MPC enhancements, and mandatory 2FA for all accounts. Our self-developed matching engine ensures order integrity.

Explore Security → 📋

Compliance & Regulation

U.S. MSB registered with FinCEN since Oct 2021. We adhere to BSA/AML frameworks, FATF Travel Rule guidelines, KYC/sanctions screening, and localized compliance in each jurisdiction.

Explore Compliance → ⚡

Products & Services

Spot and C2C trading, institutional brokerage and custody solutions, batch settlement tools, REST/WebSocket/FIX APIs, and tiered fee structures designed for individuals and institutions alike.

Explore Products → 💬

Transparent Communication

We follow a four-part disclosure model: Facts, Context, Boundaries, Follow-up. Status pages, event timelines, and root cause summaries are publicly accessible at all times.

Key Milestones

Our Journey

From project initiation to institutional-grade infrastructure — a consistent roadmap built on transparency.

Oct 2020
Project initiated — technical and operational roadmap established with "security and transparency as first principles."
Mar 2021
Alpha matching engine and strategic risk control prototype completed; closed testing commenced.
Oct 21, 2021
U.S. MSB registration completed with FinCEN. KYC/AML and sanctions screening processes fully implemented.
Feb 2022
Spot trading Beta launched; cold/hot wallet v1 deployed with address reputation scoring.
Aug 2022
First institutional market makers onboarded; API v1 released with integrated risk control.
Oct 2023
Matching engine v2 and API v3 (including FIX) launched with significant latency improvements.
Oct 2024
HSM and multi-signature strategies upgraded; abnormal withdrawal delays and secondary confirmations applied.
Oct 2025
Internal Proof-of-Reserves (PoR) trial completed with method review; disclosure baseline established.
